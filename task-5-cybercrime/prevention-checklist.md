8 preventive measures are:-

Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
Do not share personal information like OTP, bank details, or passwords.
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
Do not click on unknown links or suspicious emails.
Install antivirus and keep your device updated.
Use secure websites (https) for online payments.
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or shopping.
Always log out from accounts on shared computers.
