Do’s of Social Media
Use strong passwords to keep your account safe.
Think before posting anything online.
Keep your personal information private.
Be respectful and polite to others.
Check information before sharing (avoid fake news).


Don’ts of Social Media
Don’t share your password with anyone.
Don’t talk to strangers online.
Don’t post your personal details like address, phone number, bank details.
Don’t click on unknown links.
Don’t spread fake news or rumors.
